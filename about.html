OUR STORY

A Legend built on Hopes and Flour

It all started as a small dessert shop, now has turned into a full fledgling of a bakery. For over 9 years, ButterPeach bakery has been the first thought of people when it comes to cakes and celebrations.

Our founder, Jovi woon, believed that every cake should be made with the same care you would put into a cake for your own family. That motto hasn’t changed. We still hand-select our ingredients, mix our batters by hand and decorate with an artisan's eye.

OUR MISSION

"To craft moments of joy, one slice at a time using the finest ingredients, time-honored techniques and a whole lot of love."

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones Along the Way

2007

The Beginning

Jovi Woon opens a tiny bakery on Ampang Street with a single oven and a big dream.

2010

First Award

ButterPeach bakery wins 'Best Cake Bakery' at the KL cake contest.

2012

Expansion

Moved to a larger location with a full kitchen, doubling our team and capacity.

2014

Going Custom

Launched our cake design service for weddings and corporate events.

2019

Delivery Launch

Introduced same-day delivery service, reaching customers across the entire Kuala Lumpur area.

2024

New Chapter

Opened our second location and launched the monthly cake subscription box.
